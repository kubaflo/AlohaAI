# Learning Platforms & Courses

The best places to deepen your AI knowledge beyond this app.

## Free Courses & Tutorials

- **DeepLearning.AI Short Courses** (deeplearning.ai) — Andrew Ng's platform offers bite-sized courses on specific AI topics (RAG, fine-tuning, agents, prompt engineering). Many are free.
- **Hugging Face Course** (huggingface.co/learn) — Comprehensive, hands-on courses covering NLP, diffusion models, and audio processing with real code examples
- **fast.ai** (course.fast.ai) — "Practical Deep Learning for Coders" — one of the best free deep learning courses available
- **Google AI Essentials** (grow.google/ai-essentials) — Beginner-friendly introduction to AI concepts and applications
- **Microsoft AI Skills** (learn.microsoft.com) — Free learning paths covering Azure AI, Semantic Kernel, and Copilot development

## Research & Papers

- **Papers With Code** (paperswithcode.com) — Browse state-of-the-art ML papers with linked code implementations. Great for staying current.
- **arXiv** (arxiv.org) — Preprint server where most AI research is published first
- **Distill.pub** — Beautifully visualized explanations of ML concepts (archived but still valuable)
- **The Gradient** (thegradient.pub) — Accessible AI research summaries and analysis

## Communities

- **Hugging Face Community** — Active forums and Discord for open-source AI
- **MLOps Community** (mlops.community) — Focused on production ML systems
- **AI Stack Exchange** — Q&A for AI/ML practitioners
- **r/MachineLearning** — Reddit's ML research community
- **r/LocalLLaMA** — Community focused on running LLMs locally

## Newsletters & Blogs

- **The Batch** (deeplearning.ai) — Andrew Ng's weekly AI newsletter
- **AI News** by Jack Clark — Weekly AI policy and technology updates
- **Lilian Weng's Blog** (lilianweng.github.io) — Deep technical explanations of ML concepts
- **Simon Willison's Weblog** — Practical perspectives on LLMs and AI tools

> Dedicate 30 minutes a week to reading AI research — staying current in this field is a superpower.
